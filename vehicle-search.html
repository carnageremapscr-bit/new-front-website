‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about ECU remapping and tuning

What is ECU remapping?

ECU remapping is the process of modifying the software in your vehicle's Engine Control Unit to improve performance, power output, and fuel efficiency. It adjusts parameters like fuel injection, boost pressure, and ignition timing.

Is ECU remapping safe for my vehicle?

Yes, when done professionally. Our remaps are developed within safe parameters for your engine. We always perform a full diagnostic check before tuning and ensure your vehicle is in good condition.

Will remapping void my warranty?

It depends on your manufacturer. Some warranties may be affected. We offer switchable maps that can be reverted to stock if needed for dealer visits.

What's the difference between Stage 1, 2 and 3?

Stage 1: Software-only tune for standard vehicles. Stage 2: Requires hardware upgrades like intercooler/exhaust. Stage 3: Major modifications including turbo upgrades for maximum power.

How long does a remap take?

A typical Stage 1 remap takes 1-2 hours including diagnostics. Stage 2 and 3 may take longer depending on the complexity and any required adjustments.

Do you offer mobile remapping?

Yes! We offer mobile ECU remapping across Kent and the South East. We come to your home or workplace at no extra charge within our coverage area.
