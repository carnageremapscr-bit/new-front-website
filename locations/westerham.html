Common Questions About ECU Tuning

What vehicles can be tuned?

We tune most petrol and diesel vehicles from 2000 onwards. Contact us with your vehicle details for compatibility confirmation.

Will tuning affect my warranty?

Remapping can affect manufacturer warranty. We discuss this thoroughly before proceeding and offer legal alternatives.

How much fuel economy will I lose?

Most tuned vehicles maintain similar fuel consumption despite increased power output through optimized combustion.

Can Stage tuning be reversed?

Yes, we can revert to factory settings at any time. Your original map is safely backed up throughout the process.

What about reliability after remapping?

All our tuning maps are thoroughly tested and proven reliable across thousands of vehicles nationwide.

How do you ensure quality?

Each remap is tested on our diagnostic equipment before and after to guarantee performance improvements.
