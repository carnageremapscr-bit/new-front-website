Power Tuning Questions

How much power can I actually gain?

Realistic gains range from 20-80 BHP depending on vehicle, condition, and tuning stage selected.

Are power gains permanent?

Yes, ECU maps are permanent until you choose to reverse them. No maintenance needed post-tune.

How do you prove the power gains?

We provide detailed before/after diagnostic reports showing exact power and torque improvements.

Does power tuning affect reliability?

No, properly executed power tuning maintains engine reliability with proven dyno-tested maps.

What vehicles see best power gains?

Turbo petrol and diesel engines typically see the most significant power improvements from tuning.

Can I undo power tuning?

Yes, your original ECU file is backed up and can be restored anytime at your request.
