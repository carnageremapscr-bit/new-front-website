Common Questions About ECU Remapping in Ashford

What is ECU remapping?

ECU remapping is the process of reprogramming your vehicle's engine control unit to improve performance, efficiency, and power output. We modify the ECU software to unlock your vehicle's hidden potential safely.

Is ECU remapping safe for my vehicle?

Yes! Our Stage 1 remaps are completely safe and maintain manufacturer reliability. We only push the engine to safe limits. Modern engines are designed to handle more power - we just unlock it safely.

How much power can I expect?

Stage 1 typically adds 20-40bhp and 30-60nm of torque depending on your vehicle. Stage 2 can add 40-80bhp for modified cars. We provide specific power figures for your exact vehicle model.

How long does the remap take?

Most Stage 1 remaps take 1-2 hours. We come to your location in Ashford, Tenterden, or throughout Kent. The process is completely mobile - no need to visit a garage.

Will it affect my warranty?

The remap can be reversed at any time without leaving a trace, so you maintain warranty cover if needed. However, we recommend discussing with your dealer first.

What vehicles can you remap?

We can tune almost all modern vehicles: petrol, diesel, hybrid, and electric cars. From popular models like VW, Audi, Ford, and BMW to specialist vehicles - contact us for your specific model.
