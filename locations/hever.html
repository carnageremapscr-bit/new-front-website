Diesel Tuning Information

Diesel engines respond especially well to ECU tuning, often producing 30-80 BHP gains with improved torque.

Is DPF delete recommended?

DPF removal improves power and economy but affects emissions. We advise on legal and technical aspects.

How do diesel maps differ?
