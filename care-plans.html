‚ùì Care Plan FAQs

When can I purchase a care plan?

Care plans can be purchased after your remap is complete, or added to your booking at the time of tuning. They're active for 12 months from your tuning date.

What if I change my vehicle?

Plans are vehicle-specific, but we can discuss transferring remaining benefits to a new vehicle if you get it tuned with us. Contact us to arrange.

Can I upgrade my plan mid-year?

Yes! You can upgrade to a higher tier at any time. We'll calculate the pro-rata difference and credit your remaining time.

What counts as a 'map tweak'?

A map tweak is a minor adjustment within your current tuning stage - like adjusting throttle response or fine-tuning for your driving style. Major changes (e.g., Stage 1 to Stage 2) are not included.
