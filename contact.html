Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a remap take?

Most remaps take between 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the service. We offer mobile tuning so you can stay at your location.

Will remapping void my warranty?

Remapping may affect manufacturer warranty. We can discuss the implications for your specific vehicle during consultation.

What's the difference between stages?

Stage 1 is standard ECU tuning. Stage 2 works with modifications. Stage 3 is maximum tuning. Check your vehicle using our portal.

Can you tune my vehicle?

We can tune most modern vehicles (2008+). Use our vehicle search tool to check your specific model's compatibility.

Is ECU remapping safe?

Yes, professional remapping is safe when done by experts. We use proven software and conduct diagnostics before and after.

What areas do you cover?

We provide mobile tuning across most of South East England. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.

How much power will I gain?

Gains vary by vehicle. A 2.0 TDI typically gains 40-50 BHP. Use our vehicle search to see expected gains for your model.

Do you offer guarantees?

Yes, our work is backed by professional support and care plans are available for ongoing protection and maintenance.

Can I reverse the tuning?

Yes, we can restore your vehicle to its original factory settings if needed. Just contact us for reversal services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept card payments, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment can be arranged at booking or after services.
