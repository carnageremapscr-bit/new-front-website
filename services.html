üö´ EGR & DPF Services

Exhaust Gas Recirculation & Diesel Particulate Filter Removal

EGR Service

An EGR delete is a performance part that removes the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) valve from a vehicle. The EGR valve recirculates exhaust gas back into the engine's intake system to reduce emissions.

DPF Service

DPF delete is the process of removing the Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) from a vehicle's exhaust system and reprogramming the Engine Control Unit (ECU) to work without the diesel particulate filter.
